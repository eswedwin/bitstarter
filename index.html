The power that is automatically switched off on your premises is sold back to the Power Companies during a crisis or daily peak demand to maintain net stability. This ensures that critical systems and all lights stay on. The maximum switch off period is limited to two consecutive hours in any 12 hour period. All the transactions take place using Bitcoin thus there are no transaction fees.

Do you qualify? HVAC systems are very good candidates for partial switch-off as they consume large amounts of electicity and the temperatures recover fast when switched back to full on. A supply contract between us and your company will be set up for a initial period of 3 years.

Our equipment will be installed and maintained on your premises by our installation technicians and hooked up to your systems. Power Companies:

During a crisis (line trip, line overload, generator failure, etc.) the cloud power station comes online instantaneous (less than 5 seconds) to make up the shortfall and then keep on generating for up to 2 hours. It can also be used with tidal power or wind-generators to compensate for sudden dips in the supply.

The Power Companies pay a facility fee of less than 4% of the cost of a similar sized new base-load generation plant over the three year contract plus a fairly steep price for the energy that is comparable to the cost for peaking PowerStations. The Power Companies recover some of the money by reselling the power to you after the crisis or peak at a lower rate. (All envisaged are recoverable loads.)

FAQ

What does HVAC stand for?
Heating Ventilation and Air-Conditioning.
Why must the grid supply exactly match the demand?
The energy generated in the grid (network) must exactly balance the sum of the energy losses and demand. If the generated energy is higher the generators all speed up causing the frequency and voltage of the grid to increase. When it is lower the frequency and voltage decreases as the generators cannot keep up and thus slow down. As all electric motors run at, or nearly at, the same frequency they also slow down causing the demand to increase and a runaway condition to ensue.
